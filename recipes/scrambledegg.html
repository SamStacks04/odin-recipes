Scrambled Eggs

Description

Super quick and fluffy scrambled eggs!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Combine eggs, butter, milk, and salt into a bowl and mix, then let rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Pour egg mix into pan then turn to high heat.
  3. Stir egg mix with a spatula very frequently, forming clumps. Every minute that goes by stir the mix for 30 seconds while taking the pan off the stove.
  4. Once the eggs are just slightly more moist than desired, turn off the stove and let the eggs cook themselves until perfection
  5. Serve on a plate and enjoy
