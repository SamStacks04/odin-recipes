Steak

Description

A perfectly cooked medium rare steak.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme

Preparation

  1. Pat steaks dry and liberally season with salt
  2. In a cast iron skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat
  3. Once hot, transfer steaks onto skillet. Sear each side for 3-4 minutes. Afterwards, use tongs to sear the edges for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Reduce heat to medium, and add in the butter, garlic, and herbs.
  5. Continously spoon the butter sauce until the steak is about 10 degrees away from the desired temperature.
